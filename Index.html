My Story

A versatile cellist and Juilliard graduate, Talia held a leadership position in a major symphony orchestra before the COVID-19 pandemic. When the virus caused her and countless other professional musicians to lose their jobs, she began studying user experience and design interaction. Now she is helping people adapt to virtual platforms in a world that continues to be socially distanced for the foreseeable future.

In addition to UX, Talia enjoys learning languages, reading horror stories, skit-writing, and playing with her cats.


How my background sets me apart in the design world

Music has taught me how to find beauty, nuance, and the path to the heart. It has also left me with some incredible soft skills, such as:

attention to detail
discipline and astounding work ethic
skills in active listening and constructive criticism
collaboration, public speaking, stage presence
experience working with people from many different countries and cultures
a desire to find fun and better work environments
how to self-promote and market
how to emotionally move an audience
